# Fix Planning And Prioritization

## Priority rubric

- `P0`: blocks completion or breaks service promise
- `P1`: major friction, delay, or repeated manual rescue
- `P2`: inconsistency or inefficiency without full blockage

## Planning tracks

1. quick repairs
2. structural fixes
3. validation instrumentation

## Output shape

```
Quick repairs:
1. Action: ...
   - Owner: ...
   - ETA: ...

Structural fixes:
1. Action: ...
   - Owner group: ...
   - Dependency: ...
```
